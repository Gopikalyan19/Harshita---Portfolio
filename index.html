Publications

Unravelling the Energy Harvesting Performance of Antimony doped BaTiO3 towards Self-powered On-body Wearable Impact Sensor
This study focuses on harvesting ambient mechanical energy using antimony-doped barium titanate (BST) nanoparticles blended with PDMS to create a flexible composite piezoelectric nanogenerator (FCF-PENG). The device generates a peak output of 28V and 1.5A, with a power density of 1.6mW/m. Integrated into a wearable impact sensor, it detects collisions and monitors in real-time using ESP32 and ThingSpeak cloud, with potential applications in sports healthcare and rehabilitation with IoT integration.
Publication date: Mar 5, 2025
